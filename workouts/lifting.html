Weightlifting


Lifting weights, like anything else in life, can have it's own learning curve to chase. There are many ways in which one can lift weights. There are subcategories such as bodybuilding, specific strength training, or even for rehabilitation.


My own preferred way to lift weights is geared towards specific strength training. I began lifting weights as a teenager for HS sports such as football and track. Theses sports each required specific methods of training. Nowadays, I dedicate my training towards that of distance running. Mainly to help strengthen and prevent injury during marathon preps.
