Group Workout

There has been a rise in group workout classes over the past decade and it shows no stops of slowing down. There can be a group workout for just about any style of working out. Yoga, run clubs, group HIIT classes, pilates and so much more! This variety and availability are what spark community within group workouts.


Personally, I was always a bit skeptical about group workouts. I had always been someone that liked to workout alone. Then one day I tried a HIIT class with a friend and I instantly became a fan! With some good music, a passionate instructor and like-minded people, it's hard not to enjoy a class.
