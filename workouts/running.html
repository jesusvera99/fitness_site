Running


Running is the tried and true physical activity that has almost no entry barrier. Running, just like walking is a skill that comes naturally to the human physiology. Think about centuries ago when humans had to run/hunt for their dinner. It is natural for a human to run.


Nowadays, just about anyone can run anytime, anywhere. It is a favorite of mine for the sole reason that I can simply lace up my shoes, step outside and run where I please. This very reason is one of the many reasons that I continue to chase physical achievment within running.
